To create a Postman collection for the given APIs, you can follow these steps:

1. Open Postman and click on the "New" button to create a new collection.
2. Give your collection a name, for example, "Project Cloud S5 Backend".
3. Click on the "Create" button to create the collection.
4. Inside the collection, click on the "Add request" button to create a new request.
